SURVIVAL GAME

How to Play

  • This game is about the survival of the sheep herd.
  • You are given a herd of sheep represented by the green color and wolves represented by the red color.
  • After every 5 seconds, the herd of sheep increases by 5 pixels of radius and the wolves increase by one.
  • If you get hit by a wolf, the radius of the sheep will decrease by 5 pixels.
