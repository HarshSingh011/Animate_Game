SURVIVAL GAME

How to Play

  • This game is about the survival of the sheep herd.
  • You are given a herd of sheep represented by the green color and wolves represented by the red color.
  • You can move herd of sheep by just moving your mouse.
  • After every 3 seconds, the herd of sheep increases and the wolves are increase by one.
  • If you get hit by a wolf, the radius of the sheep will decrease.
  • Each increase in wolf shows increase in level of game and at the end your score will be provided.
